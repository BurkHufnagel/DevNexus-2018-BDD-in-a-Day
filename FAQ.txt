BDD-in-a-Day Frequently Asked Questions

1) Why is it called "BDD in a Day" when it's only a half-day (4 hour) workshop.
      There intent is that will be two versions of this workshop. A half-day version for developers who want to focus the more technical parts
      and a full day version that includes practices to determine which features provide the most value, etc.
